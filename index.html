Welcome!

Hello! My name is Justin Nipales and I am currently
a student at College of Southern Nevada and want
to showcase a few of my hobbies.

Exploring New Cultures

I have always been interested in trying different types of food. I have always been exposed to different types of food in my life and I am always open to trying new foods. I have also gone to many different places in the world, including some places like the Philippines, Jamaica, and Hawaii. I even love making new types of food. Just seeing what new types of food I am able to try just lets me know how the culture of that specific food is like and how one day I may be able to travel there and try it.

Playing Kendama

I play a Japanese skill toy called kendama and the culture around it is amazing. I have been playing kendama for about 9 years now. The community that has been built around this one single ball and cup toy has helped a lot of people create new friends and even travel to new places. At one event I have went to, I was fortunate enough to place second in a freestyle competition and win some prizes along with it. I have loved kendama ever since I started playing and will keep continuing to play with it.

Digital Drawing

I started to pick up drawing not too long ago and have enjoyed making little emojis or emotes for a website called Twitch. Ever since the pandemic started I have been watching a lot of Twitch and even tried my hand at streaming on there. I was very fortunate that I was able to make friends on this website and join many communities. I wanted to throw my hat in the ring for drawing emotes because of how you are able to do so much customization to a single emote and transform it in many different ways.
